Consider the algebraic form:

\begin{equation*} f(x) = \dfrac{\sqrt{3x-4}}{x+5} \end{equation*}

Sketch a graph of \(y_1=\sqrt{3x-4}\) and another graph of \(y_2=x+5\text{.}\) How might each output of \(y_1\) change by getting divided by each output of \(y_2\text{?}\)

Now convert \(f(x)\) into numerical form and plot the points on a graph. Does the image make sense based on what you see of \(y_1\) and \(y_2\text{?}\)
