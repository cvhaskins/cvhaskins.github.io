A linear equation is an equation which sets two linear expressions equal to each other. The linear equation may have a single variable, like

\begin{equation*} 3(x-5)+2x = \dfrac{1}{2}x - 5 \end{equation*}

or have multiple variables like

\begin{equation*} y=5x+6 \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} 2x = 3y-7x + 5 \end{equation*}
